Creating the sample application, Whiteboard app

The objectives of the app are:

  1. Maintain a simple shared to-do list
  2. Add, remove items
  3. Move them up and down
  4. Assign to someone
  5. Share the link with others
  6. Allow others to edit (if they are logged in)
  7. Allow others to register

The app will be a single page app, with a list of items and a list of members.

Actions:

  1. Add item
  2. Remove item
  3. Move item
  4. Add user
  5. Remove user
  6. Sign in and sign out
